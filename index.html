Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: August 26, 2025

Welcome to Person Pilot, owned and operated by HashBaze. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mood tracking app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information:

  • Name, email address when you create an account
  • Photos you choose to capture or upload for mood tracking
  • Mood entries, reflections, and thoughts you record in the app
  • Usage patterns and app activity data

1.2 Device Information:

  • Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • IP address and general location information
  • App usage statistics and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide mood tracking and reflection features
  • Generate personalized insights and recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important updates about the app
  • Provide customer support when needed

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these situations:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate the app (cloud storage, analytics) under strict confidentiality
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect user safety
  • With Your Consent: Only when you explicitly authorize sharing

4. Your Rights

You can:

  • Update your personal information through the app settings
  • Delete your account and data by contacting our support
  • Control photo and camera access through your device settings
  • Export your mood data before deletion

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no system is 100% secure. We recommend using strong passwords and keeping your device secure.

Your mood data and photos are encrypted and stored securely on our servers.

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we securely remove your personal data within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Person Pilot is not intended for users under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you're a parent and believe your child has provided information, please contact us immediately.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use of the app means you accept any updates.
